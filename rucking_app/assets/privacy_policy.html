GRY (Go Ruck Yourself) - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how GRY ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and username. Optionally, you may provide your weight, height, date of birth, and gender to enhance workout calculations.
  • Workout Data: We collect data related to your rucking sessions, including distance, duration, pace, heart rate, calories, and elevation changes.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location data during your rucking sessions to track your route and calculate distance and pace.
  • Camera Access: With your permission, we access your device's camera to allow you to take and upload profile pictures and share workout achievements.
  • Health Data: With your permission, we may access health data from your device's health app to enhance workout tracking.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Track and analyze your rucking sessions
  • Calculate calories burned and other fitness metrics
  • Allow you to share your achievements with other users (if you choose)
  • Communicate with you about your account and provide customer support
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information when you direct us to do so, such as when you choose to share your workouts with other users.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who need access to your information to provide services to us, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share information if required to comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes.

Your Choices

You can manage your privacy preferences through the App's settings, including:

  • Updating or deleting your account information
  • Controlling location tracking permissions
  • Managing camera access permissions
  • Controlling health data integration
  • Adjusting sharing preferences for your workout data

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete this information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
